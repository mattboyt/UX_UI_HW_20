CarMD

A CAR MAINTENANCE APP FOR VEHICLE OWNERS

About The Project

Since inflation for car-related goods are among the highest since 1980, taking care of your car is more important now more than ever. Car maintenance can be daunting for car owners who do not have a lot of experience or limited income. Our app offers a sense of security and confidence to be able to maintain and plan for costly car repairs in the future.

Problem Statement

A young nurse lacks the bandwidth, confidence and knowledge to learn vehicle maintenance, and the ability to budget for car repairs. She also feels distrust for mechanics who tend to up-sell on unnecessary or overpriced car repairs. How might we improve her experience so she can get trusted information and plan for current and potential incidents?
